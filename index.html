Support & Privacy

Get help with FocusMind and learn how we handle your data — spoiler: it never leaves your device.

App support

How do I upgrade to the paid version?

Open FocusMind, go to Settings → Upgrade and choose the plan that suits you. The purchase is handled securely through the App Store.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings → Restore purchases. Make sure you're signed in to the same Apple ID you used when you originally purchased. If the problem persists, contact us.

I lost my data — can you recover it?

All your data is stored only on your device. We have no access to it and cannot recover it remotely. We recommend enabling iCloud Backup in your iPhone settings so your app data is included in your regular device backups.

How do I move my data to a new iPhone?

Use Apple's standard device transfer process (iCloud backup or direct transfer via cable). As long as you restore from a backup that includes FocusMind, all your data will carry over automatically.

The app is crashing — what should I do?

Try force-quitting the app and reopening it. If that doesn't help, restart your iPhone. Still crashing? Send us an email with your device model and iOS version and we'll investigate.

How do I delete all my data?

Simply delete the app from your iPhone. Since everything is stored locally on your device, uninstalling FocusMind removes all associated data completely.

Privacy policy

FocusMind is built with a simple principle: your data belongs to you. Everything you create stays on your device. We never collect, transmit, or have access to any of your personal information.

What data we collect

None. FocusMind does not collect any personal data, usage data, analytics, or crash reports. No information ever leaves your device.

Where your data is stored

All data you create in FocusMind — tasks, notes, settings, and any other content — is stored exclusively on your iPhone or iPad using Apple's local storage. We have no servers, no databases, and no access to your content.

Third-party services

FocusMind does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. The only external service involved is Apple's App Store, which handles payment processing for the premium version. Apple's own privacy policy governs that transaction.

In-app purchases

FocusMind offers a free version and a paid upgrade. All purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information — Apple handles this entirely.

Children's privacy

Because we collect no data whatsoever, FocusMind is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Any future changes will be reflected here before they take effect.
