Privacy policy

How we handle your data

The short version: FocusMind collects absolutely nothing. No personal data, no analytics, no tracking of any kind. Everything you create stays on your device and is only ever accessible by you.

What data we collect

None. FocusMind does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data, usage statistics, analytics, or crash reports. No information of any kind is ever sent from your device to us or to any third party.

Where your data lives

All content you create in FocusMind — your tasks, notes, settings, and anything else — is stored exclusively on your iPhone or iPad using Apple's local storage. We have no servers, no databases, and no access to your content whatsoever.

Third-party SDKs and services

FocusMind contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking code. The only external service involved in your use of FocusMind is Apple's App Store, which processes payments for the premium upgrade. Apple's own privacy policy governs that transaction — we never receive or see your payment information.

In-app purchases

FocusMind offers a free version and a one-time paid upgrade, processed entirely by Apple via StoreKit. We receive confirmation that a purchase was made so we can unlock your features, but we never receive your name, billing address, card details, or any other payment information.

Children's privacy

Because we collect no data from anyone, FocusMind is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. There is nothing to collect, share, or misuse.

Your rights

Since we hold no personal data about you, there is nothing for us to provide, correct, or delete on our end. If you want to remove all your data, simply delete the app from your device — that's all it takes.

Changes to this policy

If we ever update this policy, the date at the top of this page will change. We will not introduce any form of data collection without clearly communicating it here first.
